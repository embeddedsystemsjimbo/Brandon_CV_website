About Myself

Over the past decade I was a certified welder and a licensed automotive technician. I worked both with technologies and with my own hands in creating, diagnosing, repairing, and providing various automotive and mechanical products at both industrial and home-used scales. My experience with vehicle electronics and powertrain management systems led me to complete formal coursework in the Engineering field where I have recently acquired my bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering from Ryerson University. I am passionate about new technology with special interests in computer architecture, digital signal processing and embedded systems and would relish the opportunity to apply my engineering capabilities to a broad range of engineering projects.
